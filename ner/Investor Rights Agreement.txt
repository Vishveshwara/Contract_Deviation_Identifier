Title: Investor Rights Agreement

Parties Involved:

Company: [Company Name], a [corporation/partnership/LLC] organized under the laws of [State/Country], with its principal place of business at [Address] ("Company").
Investor: [Investor Name or Company Name], with its principal place of business at [Address] ("Investor").

1. Definitions:
Common Stock: The common stock, [class of common stock], of the Company.
Preferred Stock: The preferred stock, [class of preferred stock], of the Company.
Registrable Securities: The shares of Common Stock issuable or issued on conversion of the Preferred Stock and any other securities defined as Registrable Securities in this Agreement.
Qualified IPO: A firm commitment underwritten public offering of the Company's Common Stock under a registration statement on Form S-1 (or any successor form) under the Securities Act, with a public offering price of at least [Share Price] per share, resulting in aggregate cash proceeds to the Company of at least [Minimum Company Cash Proceeds] (before payment of underwriter's discounts and commissions).
Major Investor: An Investor holding at least [500,000] shares of Registrable Securities (subject to adjustment for stock splits, stock dividends, combinations, or the like).

2. Registration Rights:
Demand Registration: Initiating Holders (holders of at least 50% of the Registrable Securities) can demand that the Company file a registration statement for their shares.
Piggyback Registration: If the Company proposes to register any of its securities, it shall offer the Investors the opportunity to include their Registrable Securities in the registration.
Form S-3 Registration: If the Company is eligible to use Form S-3, the Major Investors may request an unlimited number of registrations on Form S-3.

3. Information Rights:
Annual Financial Statements: The Company shall deliver to each Major Investor its audited balance sheet and income statement within [60] days after the fiscal year-end.
Quarterly Financial Statements: The Company shall deliver its unaudited quarterly income statement and statement of cash flows within [30] days after the end of each quarter.
Inspection Rights: Major Investors may, at their own expense, inspect the Company's properties, books, and records upon reasonable notice during normal business hours.

4. Board Representation:
Board Composition: The Company's Board shall consist of [Number of Directors] directors.
Election of Directors: The Investors shall elect [Designated Number] directors to the Board.
Board Observers: [Designating Director Name] shall appoint one or more persons to observe Board and committee meetings, with the right to participate in discussions, subject to exclusions for privilege or conflicts.

5. Right of First Offer:
Notice of New Shares: If the Company proposes to issue new shares, it shall notify each Investor, offering them the right to purchase their pro-rata portion.
Pro-rata Portion: The proportion of shares each Investor may purchase, based on the number of shares of Common Stock they hold relative to the total outstanding shares.

6. Expenses:
Reimbursement: The Company shall reimburse directors and observers for all reasonable out-of-pocket expenses incurred in attending Board and committee meetings.

7. Confidentiality:
Non-Disclosure Agreement: The parties shall be bound by the terms of the non-disclosure agreement dated [Date] and attached as [Attachment Number].

8. Indemnification:
Indemnity: The Company shall indemnify and hold harmless the Investors from any claims arising out of a breach of this Agreement, subject to limitations set forth in the Agreement.

9. Termination:
Duration: This Agreement will terminate on the earlier of (i) immediately before the closing of an Acquisition or (ii) [five] years following the closing of a Qualified IPO.

10. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution:
Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [State/Country].
Dispute Resolution: Any disputes arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved through [arbitration/mediation/litigation] in [City, State/Country].

11. Miscellaneous:
Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter hereof.
Amendment: Any amendment to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both parties.
Severability: If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

12. Signatures:
In Witness Whereof, the parties hereto have executed this Investor Rights Agreement as of the Effective Date.
